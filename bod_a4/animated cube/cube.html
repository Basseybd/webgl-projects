Controlling the cube

Scale controls

On the X axis, press the Q Key to scale up and press the A key to scale down.
On the Y axis, press the W Key to scale up and press the S key to scale down.
On the Z axis, press the E Key to scale up and press the D key to scale down.

Rotate controls

Press the X Key to rotate on X axis and the C Key in the reverse direction.
Press the V Key to rotate on Y axis and the B Key in the reverse direction.
Press the N Key to rotate on Z axis and the M Key in the reverse direction.

Translate controls

Press the I Key to move the cube up.
Press the K Key to move the cube down.
Press the J Key to move the cube left.
Press the L Key to move the cube right.
Note that since you will be using the default orthographic projection, translation in the Z direction should have no visible effect on the size of your cube. Of course if you transform the cube outside of the default view volume, it will be clipped.
Press the U Key to move the cube up on the z axis.
Press the O Key to move the cube down on the z axis.

Increase Delta

In any transformation, press the R key to increase the delta and the F key to decrease delta.

Reset

Press the 0 digit bar Key to reset the cube and delta values.
